Innovation at Your Fingertips

Our flagship utility application combines utility logic with clean, local-first architecture. It is built to adapt to you: providing personalization that fits your needs, ease of use that feels natural, and powerful features styled with aesthetic care.

🍳 Custom Element Builder

Mix and match stopwatches, countdown clocks, checklist items, and clicker counters on a single board. Adjust display density (Spacious, Compact, Dense) to fit your reading preference.

🔗 Configurable Automation Rules

Build automation without code. Connect elements so that completing checklists or hitting timer limits triggers audible alarms, tactile feedback, or changes in secondary counters.

🔒 Local-First Security

Privacy is key. Your habit, health, or laboratory records are stored locally in an encrypted database on your physical device. No accounts, no cloud sync, and no tracking cookies.

📊 Rich Timeline Ledger

Review completed logs in a detailed event history that documents ticks, tallies, and pauses down to the second.

Versatile Workflow Examples:
🍳 Culinary Prep Independent oven and stovetop timers linked to ingredients checks.
🔬 Lab Experiments Multi-instrument sample tallies tied to reaction clocks with automated increments.
🏋️ Interval Training Rep counters triggering automated rest timers and screen pulses.
💻 Sprint Tracking Task checklists linked to development compile and testing timers.
